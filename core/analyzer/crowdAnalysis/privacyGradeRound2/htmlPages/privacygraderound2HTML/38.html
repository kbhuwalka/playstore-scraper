App Description

“What? You said I was a foolish chicken? No! I’m a bird!! I will be the king of the jungle to show you that I’m a chicken! No! A BIRD!! I will make all of you look like me~~”
KEKE happened to be in a jungle and was ridiculed as a foolish chicken by other birds. KEKE wishes one day the God will help him beaten these birds.
You are now KEKE’s God who helps him, gives him food and makes his wishes come true. Remember you have to stop feeding him when KEKE is hitting the enemies, or he will be crushed to death.
The only thing you do during the game is to feed KEKE by pressing on him to help him grow bigger to beat enemies with different skills. However, you have to stop pressing when KEKE is hitting the enemies, or KEKE will die and become the enemy. Your task in the game is to make all the enemies look like KEKE, except the wood… So… , Let’s begin fighting…!!!

App Description

Poker meets Alice in Wonderland in this Strategy Role Playing Game (RPG) with lots of powerful and stunning cards to battle your enemies. Be prepared for a whole new adventure and a completely different take on the popular tale in Alice of Hearts.

As the late evening roles in, Alice closes the café for the night and bids her colleague goodnight. She begins her usual walk home, when a sound coming from a nearby alley distracts her. Curiosity gets the better of Alice, and she ends up being faced with a challenge she could never have imagined.

Follow Alice through Wonderland as she struggles to find her way home. Familiar faces trick and tease her for their own gains, but they have made one mistake; they have underestimated Alice.

With her trusted Ego warriors fighting alongside her, Alice is ready for the toughest of tests.

Innovative Card-Base Battles:
- Battle foes using poker hands, skills and other abilities
- Place better hands for greater effect; Royal Flush!
- Use the skills that your battle companion; the Ego possesses
- Collect rare Special Cards that can help change the tide of the battle

Ego Creation System
- Use the warriors of Wonderland; Egos to aid you in your battle
- Create Egos with different strengths and abilities to combat every challenge
- Arm your Egos with weapons, items and armour, and Level them up through combat
- Hundreds of combinations to choose from, find the ones which work best for you

Multiplayer Challenge
- Turn-Based play that lets you return to the battle at your convenience
- Take your Ego into battle against other players
- Challenge your friends through Facebook
- Fight your way to the top of the Leaderboard

App Description

Enjoy the best visual effect; as good as you play on PC! This game is very similar to Zuma's Revenge and Luxor!
'Marble Blast - Zodiac Saga [Online]' is a Zuma-like (marble shoot out, match 3) game with the theme of Norse mythology. It is easy to play, but truly addictive.
Your goal is to clear all the marbles before they reach the end of the path, and meanwhile, achieve Combos, Chains and Gaps as many as possible to get the highest score.
#The online multiplayer mode requires Google Play Game Service and Google +. Please upgrade your Google Play Store and Google+ to the latest version.#
How to play:
1. Touch the area of the screen where you want to shoot.
2. Group 3 or more identical marbles together to clear them.
3. Swap the shooting marble by touching the marble emitter.
4. Achieve Chains by clearing marbles continuously using shooting.
5. Achieve Combos by clearing marbles continuously using attracting.
6. Achieve Gaps by clearing distant marbles using shooting.
7. Get a higher score by achieving Combos, Chains and Gaps as many as possible.
Features of Online Mode:
1. No need to create an additional account – Simply sign in with Google+ and play with your friends!
2. Voice Chat! - You can chat with your friends both in text and voice!
3. Challenge your friends! – Send an invitation to your friends. The game will start automatically after the invitation being accepted.
4. Try to get a higher score by achieving Combos (+2), Chains (+1) and Gaps (+3) as many as possible; the player who gets 100 score first will win the game.
Features of Classic Mode:
1. Very Easy to Play - Suitable for all age groups.
2. Large variety of Beautiful Scenes - 6 different scenes with 216 well designed levels!
3. Flexible Gameplay - This game contains the gameplay of the original zuma game (Zuma's Revenge) and the Loxur series, which will offer you more fun.
4. Attractive Props - Lightning ball, multicolor ball and more interesting props are available.
5. Growing System – You may reduce the difficulty level by upgrading the marbles with coins you earned in the game.
6. Customized Levels - You can create levels all by yourself!

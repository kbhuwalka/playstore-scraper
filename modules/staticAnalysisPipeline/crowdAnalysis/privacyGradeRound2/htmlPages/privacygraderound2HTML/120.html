App Description

The official Android app of the most popular soccer game: Online Soccer Manager (also known as OSM).

If you like free soccer games, you're going to love playing Online Soccer Manager. Pick your favourite soccer team, choose your line-up, decide on your tactics and compete against your soccer manager friends!

★ Definitely a five star quality soccer game;
★ Over 4 million active soccer managers worldwide and still growing strong;
★ An exciting experience for everyone who enjoys soccer.

As a soccer manager you need to determine which formation suits your squad, or buy soccer players to suit your favourite formation. Make sure your soccer players are playing at their best position and win the championship!

FEATURES
- Set or change your line-up, formation and tactics;
- Buy and sell players;
- Check your match results with detailed match statistics;
- Extremely simple and easy-to-learn interface;
- Clear overviews: fixtures, season calendar, league standings and much more;
- Expand your soccer stadium;
- Play in the same league as your friends;
- Set your captain and set-piece takers;
- Check the transfer list with the best soccer players from all over the world;
- Go on a training camp and schedule a secret training;
- Hire coaches and doctors to train and treat your soccer players;
- Hire a Spy to find out what your opponent is up to.

PLEASE NOTE
This is a one season game. At the end of the season you will have to select a new team for the next one. This way you're able to manage a lot of different teams over time, which makes the game more fun and more varied compared to other soccer manager games. All your results will be stored in your profile.

When installing our App, we will ask you for permission to receive and send text messages. This is ONLY used when you decide to use an in-game payment option.

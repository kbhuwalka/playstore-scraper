App Description

*** Dont forget to go into settings if the game has bad tilt handling! ***

Simple, addictive plateform game!
if you like Mega Jump, NinJump, Hype Jump, Doodle Jump or Froggy Jump, you'll love Extreme Droid Jump!

4 game modes
- classic: Help Droid to go higher and catch bonuses!
- survival: avoid blue screen of death, and catch bonuses
- falldown: Help droid to climb down! beware of the laser line on top which is appearing at some points!
-classic kidz: as classic, but bugdroid cant die! this mode is for toddlers ;)

Tilt to move, press menu to pause.
